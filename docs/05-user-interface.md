### Action Items

Each section is clickable and expands to show full list:

1. **Renewals Due:** Accounts with renewal date in next 7 days
2. **IDs Expiring:** Recipients with ID expiring in next 30 days
3. **Turning 18:** Minor recipients with 18th birthday in next 30 days
4. **Overdue Accounts:** Past renewal date, not yet on Hold
5. **On Hold:** Currently holding mail due to non-payment
