## 12. Success Metrics

### Operational Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Account lookup time | < 10 seconds | Time from search to view |
| Renewal reminder compliance | > 95% | SMS sent on time |
| ID expiration compliance | > 98% | Valid IDs on file |
| Hold rate | < 5% | Accounts reaching Hold status |

### Business Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Revenue retention | > 95% | Renewals completed vs. due |
| Customer churn | < 10% annually | Voluntary closures |
| Average account age | Increasing | Months since open date |

### System Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| System uptime | > 99.5% | Monthly availability |
| Backup success | 100% | Daily backup completion |
| SMS delivery rate | > 98% | Delivered vs. sent |

## 13. Future Considerations

### Potential Enhancements

1. **Customer Portal:** Self-service portal for customers to view their account, update contact info, and pay online

2. **Online Payments:** Integration with payment processor for credit card payments

3. **Email Notifications:** Option for email reminders in addition to SMS

4. **Multi-Branch Support:** Expand to support multiple PostNet locations with centralized management

5. **Package Notifications:** Integration with package tracking system for arrival notifications

6. **Mobile App:** Native mobile app for staff with barcode scanning for quick lookup

7. **Reporting Dashboard:** Advanced analytics with trends and forecasting

8. **Document Storage:** Scan and store copies of IDs and forms electronically

### Integration Opportunities

- QuickBooks/accounting software for payment sync
- Twilio alternatives (for SMS cost optimization)
- Google Workspace (for calendar integration)
