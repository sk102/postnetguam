## User Personas

### Manager (Primary Admin)

**Profile:** Branch manager or owner responsible for overall operations

**Needs:**
- Full access to all system functions
- Ability to manage staff accounts
- Access to reports and analytics
- Control over rate schedules
- View audit logs for accountability

**Key Tasks:**
- Record and manage payments
- Open and close accounts
- Add/remove recipients
- Generate reports
- Manage system configuration

---

### Staff Member

**Profile:** Front-desk employee handling day-to-day customer interactions

**Needs:**
- Quick account lookup
- View customer information
- Add notes/memos to accounts
- See daily task list

**Key Tasks:**
- Look up customer accounts
- Verify recipient authorization
- Add memos to accounts
- View (but not modify) account details

---

### Customer (Indirect User)

**Profile:** Mailbox renter who receives communications from the system

**Interactions:**
- Receives SMS renewal reminders
- Receives SMS payment overdue notices
- Receives SMS ID expiration reminders
